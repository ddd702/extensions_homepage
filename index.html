📸 SnapPage

The elegant, lightweight full-page scrolling screenshot tool.

Features

  • Seamless Stitching: Pixel-level automated canvas scrolling and stitching.
  • Side Panel Workspace: Manage and preview your history gallery non-intrusively.
  • 100% Local & Private: Processes everything on-device with zero server uploads.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 10, 2026

SnapPage is committed to protecting user privacy. This extension adheres to a strict privacy-first architecture:

  • No Data Collection: We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal data, browsing history, or website content to external servers.
  • Local Storage: All captured images and history metadata are processed and stored strictly within the user's local browser database (IndexedDB) via unlimitedStorage.
  • Permissions: Permissions like activeTab and scripting are requested solely to measure dimensions and execute scrolling actions on demand.
