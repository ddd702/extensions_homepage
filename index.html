ScreenCapture - Capture and Manage Screenshots

A simple tool for capturing screenshots of web pages and saving them to a local database for later management.

Features

  • Seamless Stitching: Pixel-level automated canvas scrolling and stitching.
  • Side Panel Workspace: Manage and preview your history gallery non-intrusively.
  • 100% Local & Private: Processes everything on-device with zero server uploads.
